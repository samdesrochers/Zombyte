Zombyte

Zombyte is a retro android zombie game developped for the 3rd edition of the Great Canada Appathon (Retro theme).

Disclaimer

The Great Canadian Appathon (GCA) is a 48-hour mobile game development hackathon. We developed this game for the android platform from A to Z during one weekend including game engine, graphics and gameplay. Our team finished in 3rd place out of over 125 submissions.

Features

  • Multiple Weapons
  • Multiple Levels
  • Online Mode (Multiplayer)
  • Score Database
